## Conference overview (in progress)

As our lab is highly interdisciplinary, we attend conferences in multiple fields. Conferences that Ewald lab members have attended recently include:

* General toxicology conferences: EUROTOX, SOT, SETAC
* Others: ICEM, QSAR, ISMB, NeurIPS, SBI2
* Many local meetings on the Wellcome Genome Campus and in Cambridge

Ewald Lab members based at EBI and with contracts >1 year can expect to attend at least one international conference per year. Jess may suggest that you attend particular conferences if she receives relevant invitations, but ultimately it is up to you to bring up conference attendance during 1:1 meetings and make plans accordingly.

## Timing

Attending conferences involves multiple deadlines, including for abstract submission, registration, booking travel, and presentation preparation (both oral presentation slides and posters). Please complete each of these things **two weeks** before they are due. None can be done at the last minute: co-authors must review abstracts and posters, Jess must ensure that there is sufficient budget, presentation slides may require editing, the research office needs time to book travel, and printing a poster is a whole procedure that requires approval from multiple people (see details below).
